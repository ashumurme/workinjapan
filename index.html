Do Jobs in Japan, Pay Well

People in Japan generally work for 40 hours per week, and the minimum salary in Japan is decided by the regional Minimum Wage Council in the country. The average minimum salary in Japan is 931 JPY (USD). Also, those employees who work more than their working hours get overtime pay as decided by the Labour Standards Act of 1947. The minimum salary in Japan varies from one location to another.
